Welcome to the Portable LCE Docs!

‍A cross-platform port of Minecraft Legacy Console Edition

Portable LCE is a modified version of the Minecraft Console Legacy Edition aimed on porting old Minecraft to different platforms (such as Linux, Android, Emscripten, etc.) and refactoring the codebase to improve organization and use modern C++ features.
